Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Wed, 22 Apr 2026 11:32 IST

1. Introduction

Our Habit Tracking app (the "App") is built with a Privacy-by-Design philosophy. We believe your habits and personal progress should be your business, and yours alone. This policy explains why we do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. No Accounts: You are not required to create an account or provide an email address to use the App. Local Storage: All habit data, logs, and progress history are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. No Tracking: We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any tracking technologies to monitor your behavior across other apps or websites.

3. Use of Information

Since we do not collect any data, we cannot use, sell, or rent your information to third parties. Your data remains under your control at all times.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use certain system-level services provided by Apple (such as iCloud for backups or App Store for purchases). iCloud: If you enable iCloud syncing, your data is stored in your private Apple iCloud account. We cannot access this data. Apple Analytics: We may receive "de-identified" or aggregate technical data through Apple’s standard App Store Connect analytics (e.g., app crashes or install counts), but this does not identify you personally.

5. Compliance with Global Laws

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) As we do not process personal data of EU residents, we comply with the GDPR’s principle of Data Minimization. There is no data for you to "request access to" or "delete" from our servers because we never held it in the first place. Your "Right to Erasure" is fulfilled by simply deleting the App or clearing the App data from your iPhone settings. CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) We do not "Sell" or "Share" personal information as defined by the CCPA. We do not maintain profiles on our users. COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) Because we collect no personal information, our App is inherently compliant with COPPA and safe for all age groups.

6. Security

Your data is protected by your device’s security features (FaceID, TouchID, Passcode) and Apple’s iOS sandbox environment, which prevents other apps from accessing your Habit Tracker data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are made signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
