Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Our Commitment: This app is built with a Privacy-by-Design philosophy. We do not store, sell, or use your personal data. Your data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Our Habit Tracking app (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains why we do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

2. No Data Collection

  • No Accounts: You are not required to create an account or provide an email address to use the App.
  • Local Storage: All habit data, logs, and progress history are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your behavior.

3. Use of Information

Since we do not collect any data, we cannot use, sell, or rent your information to third parties. Your data remains under your absolute control.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses standard Apple iOS system-level services:

  • iCloud: If you enable iCloud syncing, your data is stored in your private Apple account. We cannot access it.
  • Analytics: We may receive anonymous technical data (like crash reports) through Apple App Store Connect, which does not identify you personally.

5. Compliance with Global Laws

GDPR & CCPA

We comply with the GDPR and CCPA by practicing Data Minimization. Since we do not hold your data on our servers, your right to erasure is exercised by simply deleting the App from your device.

COPPA (Children’s Privacy)

Our App collects no personal information, making it fully compliant with COPPA and safe for all ages.

6. Security

Your data is protected by your device’s native security (FaceID, TouchID, and Passcode) and Apple’s iOS sandboxing technology.
